# Code of Conduct

This project exists to help learners study without another paywall. Keep discussion respectful, practical, and focused on improving the tool.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and assume good intent.
- Give specific, actionable feedback.
- Respect beginners and non-native English speakers.
- Keep contributions legal, original, and source-checkable.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Real exam questions, braindumps, leaked content, or copied paid material.
- Spam, self-promotion unrelated to the project, or misleading pass guarantees.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate these expectations. Repeated or severe behavior can result in being blocked from the project.
